Quick Start

What To Do First

4 steps
1

Wake the audio engine

Click Wake Audio Engine once so the browser allows WebAudio to run.

2

Connect a source

For guitar, click Detect Inputs, choose your interface, then press Start Live Input. Or upload a clip instead.

3

Build the rig

Add one effect at a time, then drag the cards in the pedalboard to change the order.

4

Tweak the sound

Click any effect card to edit its controls, then use Compare Dry Signal to hear the difference.
